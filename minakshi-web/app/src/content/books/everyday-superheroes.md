# About the Book

**Superheroes**  don’t always wear capes or carry weapons. They don’t always fly. Some superheroes wear masks and protective suits. Some carry stethoscopes and thermometers. And some look through microscopes and examine lab reports. A book on everyday heroes in the time of a pandemic.

The book has been translated into 10 Indian and international languages, reaching young readers across the world and inspiring them to recognise the power of empathy and resilience in everyday acts.

## Key Milestones

The book has been translated into 10 Indian and international languages, reaching young readers across the world.

### Bookaroo Children’s Literature Festival | 2022
In 2022, Everyday Superheroes travelled to the Bookaroo Children’s Literature Festival, where it introduced young readers to stories of courage, kindness, and everyday acts of heroism.
